What We Do

The PTA organises fundraising events and activities that aim to raise up to £10,000 a year. All the money that the PTA raises goes directly back to school to support the development and educational needs of the children and staff.

Over the past few years the PTA has funded lots of exciting projects. These have included:

An outdoor classroom with sail canopy

Planting for the Forest School area

Playground equipment

Staging for school productions

Nine new Street Trees planted

Audio mixing desk

Plus sound and staging equipment for school productions, street trees, playground toys and so much more.

Who We Are

The PTA is run entirely by parent and staff volunteers. Every year, our volunteers give their free time to keep the PTA running, raising thousands of pounds (even during lockdown!) to help support our school.

For the details of our current committee members and volunteers, elected at our AGM in May 2021, please click here.

Join Us!

We are always looking for new volunteers to join and support the PTA. Every member of our school community is automatically a member of the PTA and we always need new and creative fundraising ideas to raise as much money as possible.

You can support the PTA in a number of ways including:

  • Attend our termly meetings, announced here and on Facebook
  • Come to our second-hand uniform sales and other fundraising events during the year, or volunteer to help run them
  • Set up a regularly monthly donation
  • Donate whilst you shop online through Easy Fundraising or Amazon Smile
  • See if your employer offers 'match funding' or 'match giving' – schemes where they will match any funding you raise for the PTA
  • Send us your fundraising ideas!
