Help us race to the moon!

The children of Coopers Lane have 4 weeks to race to the moon, and will get there by fuelling their tube train space ship with active travel.

Which class will reach the moon first? How much money can we raise for the school? And will the children beat the teachers?

The Moonshot Challenge is open to all Coopers Lane students, who will fuel our spaceship by cycling, scooting, walking, skating and wheeling. Here's how to take part:

  • Sign up using the form below to participate
  • We will send you details of how to get set up, track your activities and get sponsored
  • We will update the leaderboard weekly so you can see which classes - or teachers - are in the lead!

Sign Up Today!

To sign up, please enter your email address below - once per family is all that's required. We will send you an email with all the details, including how to set up your phone to track your active journeys, and then keep you updated every week.

By signing up you agree to receive emails from Coopers Lane PTA. Our emails are solely in support of fundraising for the school, we won't share your details with anyone else, and you can unsubscribe at any time.
